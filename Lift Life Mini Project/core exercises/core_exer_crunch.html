CRUNCHES EXERCISE

Crunches are a classic abdominal exercise that targets the rectus abdominis, obliques, and transverse abdominis, strengthening the core and improving stability.

STEP-BY-STEP EXPLANATION:

  1. Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor, hip-width apart.
  2. Place your hands behind your head, lightly touching your temples.
  3. Lift your upper body off the floor, curling your spine towards your hips.
  4. Lower your upper body back to the starting position, but don't fully rest your back on the floor.
